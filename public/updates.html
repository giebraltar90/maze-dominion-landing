Development Updates

Every change to the free prototype, documented as we build toward Steam. Play the prototype

Warbands & Battle Doctrine Major

July 8, 2026 · V4.4
  • Warband decks: in the waiting room you now choose your hero AND the 4 units you take into battle. Your send bar, hotkeys (Q/W/E/R) and strategy follow your pick. Bots bring themed warbands of their own.
  • Damage & armor types: arrows (Piercing) shred Unarmored and Light warbands but glance off Heavy plate and Fortified stone; cannons (Siege) crack armor but overkill nothing light; frost magic bites through Heavy plate. Every tooltip shows the matchup.
  • New tower art: all five towers were repainted with distinct silhouettes — you can read a maze at a glance now.
  • Anti-air Flak Tower is finally visible on the field (it was shy).

Heroes of the Dominion Live

July 8, 2026 · V4.3
  • Online PvP is live: host a room, send a friend the 4-letter code, they join from anywhere in the world. Deterministic lockstep keeps both battles perfectly in sync.
  • The world has names now: Baldrik Stonehand the Architect, Karvok the Ash-Crowned the Warlord, and the warbands of the Dominion — Copperfoot Couriers, Gnarlfist Breachers, Broodlings, Ruin Golems, Grimspire Gargoyles, Moonshade Shamans. Lore lines in every tooltip.
  • Heroes walk with painted 4-frame stride animations and strike a glowing spell-cast pose on every cast.
  • WC3-style edge scrolling: zoom in, push your cursor to the screen edge, the camera follows.
  • A cleaner command panel: one info strip per card, hover-reveal tier upgrades, research rows with tier pips.

The Lobby Grows Major

July 8, 2026 · V4.2
  • New game modes: 2v2 (with a bot ally), Free-for-all with 4 players, and the classic 8-player Free-for-all — your sends march on the next living enemy, last banner standing wins.
  • Watch any lane: the side panel follows your current target, or click any player in the standings to spectate them.
  • Eliminated players' mazes freeze; the battle rages on without them.

Marching Orders Live

July 8, 2026 · V4.1
  • Painted 4-frame walk cycles for Runners, Breachers, Ruin Golems and Broodlings — with footstep dust.
  • Spell effects: Instant Wall erupts from the ground, Fortify radiates golden runes, Overcharge wreathes the tower in lightning, Airstrike paints a red warning before the fireball lands.
  • Under the hood: the whole simulation became deterministic and mirror-symmetric — the foundation online PvP runs on.

Command & Conquer the Interface

July 8, 2026 · V4.0
  • Your hero and builder now muster at your home base, in front of the Undergate.
  • WC3 group control: drag a selection box or shift-click to command hero and builder together; F1/F2/F3 select them instantly.
  • Fixed a bug where upgrade, sell and hero-spell buttons silently ate clicks.
  • Unit tier upgrades (Tier I/II/III), Elo-style ranked ladder vs the bot, boss waves, comeback gold, the arena look, painted hero art, sound — the whole V3.6–V3.9 arc is documented in the GDD.

Older milestones (prototype origins, the breach mechanic, continuous LTW flow, the isometric arena) live in the Game Design Document.
